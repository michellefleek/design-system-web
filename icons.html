Usage

Phosphor Icons are included via CDN. Use the <i> tag with the appropriate class.

CDN (include per weight):

https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/@phosphor-icons/web@2.1.2/src/{weight}/style.css

HTML:

<i class="ph ph-house"></i> Regular weight
<i class="ph-bold ph-house"></i> Bold weight
<i class="ph-fill ph-house"></i> Fill weight

6 Weights

Every icon is available in 6 weights. Use the weight prefix class to switch.

Thin ph-thin
Light ph-light
Regular ph
Bold ph-bold
Fill ph-fill
Duotone ph-duotone
